EMI stands for Equated Monthly Installment. It is the fixed monthly payment you make to repay a loan over time.

EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) भनेको ऋणको भुक्तानीका लागि प्रत्येक महिना तिर्नुपर्ने निश्चित किस्ता हो।
