How to use?

  1. Select an Investment Frequency (Monthly / Quarterly / Semi-annual / Annual).
  2. Enter the Investment Amount you plan to add at the chosen frequency.
  3. Set the Expected Annual Return (%) (default is 15%). You can edit this value.
  4. Enter the Investment Period (Years).
  5. The results on the right update automatically showing Final Amount, Total Investment and Total Gains.

FAQ

What is Final Amount?

Final Amount is the maturity value including your invested capital plus estimated gains based on the inputs.

How is Total Gain calculated?

Total Gain = Final Amount - Total Investment. The calculator assumes returns are compounded at the entered annual rate and contributions are made at the selected frequency.

Can I rely on these numbers?

Values are illustrative estimates for planning purposes only and do not guarantee future returns. Consider consulting a financial advisor for personalized advice.
